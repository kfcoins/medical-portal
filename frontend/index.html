About PharmaTrust Ghana

Bridging the Gap in Ghana's Pharmaceutical Ecosystem

PharmaTrust Ghanaana is a digital platform connecting licensed pharmacists, verified medicine agents, and patients across all 16 regions of Ghana. We tackle the challenge of counterfeit drugs and medicine accessibility head-on.

Anti-Counterfeit Technology

Every medicine is verified using QR authentication before reaching patients.

Nationwide Agent Network

Active agents in Accra, Kumasi, Tamale, Cape Coast, and 12 other regions.

The Process

How PharmaTrust Ghana Works

01

Register your Pharmacy

Submit your pharmacy license, NIA card, and location. Our team verifies within 48 hours.

02

Stock Up

Order verified medicines from certified wholesalers directly via your agent dashboard.

03

Verify & Dispense

Scan QR codes to authenticate each medicine before dispensing to customers.

04

Earn & Grow

Earn commissions per order, NHIS reimbursements, and qualify for agent bonuses monthly.
